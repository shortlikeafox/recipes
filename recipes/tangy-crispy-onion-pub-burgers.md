# Tangy Crispy Onion Pub Burgers

Thick, juicy pub-style burgers with crushed fried onions and all the tang built right into the patty. No sauce needed.

**Servings:** 4
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Rest Time:** 2 minutes

---

## Ingredients

### Patties
- 1 lb 85% lean ground beef
- 3/4 cup French's crispy fried onions, crushed
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp yellow mustard
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp dill pickle juice
- 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder

### Build
- 4 burger buns (potato rolls recommended)
- 1 tbsp butter, softened
- 4 slices American cheese
- 8 pickle slices
- 1 tbsp neutral oil (canola or avocado)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Soak the onions
In a large bowl, combine the crushed fried onions, Worcestershire sauce, yellow mustard, apple cider vinegar, and pickle juice. Stir and let the crushed onions soak up the liquid for about **5 minutes**. This is where all the tang gets locked in.

### 2. Mix the patties
Add the ground beef, egg, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to the soaked onion mixture. Mix gently with your hands until just combined — do not overwork the meat. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into patties about 3/4 inch thick. Press a shallow dimple into the center of each patty with your thumb.

### 3. Toast the buns
Spread butter on the cut sides of the buns and toast in a skillet over medium heat until golden, about **2 minutes**. Set aside.

### 4. Cook the burgers
Heat a cast iron skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Add the oil. Cook the patties for about **4-5 minutes** on the first side until a deep crust forms. Flip once.

### 5. Cheese and rest
After flipping, lay a slice of American cheese on each patty. Cover with a lid or dome for about **3 minutes** to melt the cheese and finish cooking through. Pull them off and let rest for 2 minutes.

### 6. Build the burger
Place the patty on the bottom bun. Add 2 pickle slices on top. That's it — the burger does the talking.
